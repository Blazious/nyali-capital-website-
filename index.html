About Nyali Capital

Founded in 2013 by Wanjiku Wambugu, Nyali Capital Ltd was established to address the challenges faced by small businesses in accessing timely and affordable credit.

As a non-deposit-taking microfinance institution, we focus on providing short-term credit facilities, financial advisory services, and training programs to entrepreneurs across Kenya.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Nyali Capital and what services do you offer?
Nyali Capital is a non-deposit-taking microfinance institution founded in 2013. We offer LPO financing, SME working capital loans, group loans, salary advances, and financial training programs.
How long does it take to process a loan application?
We pride ourselves on fast processing times - typically within 48 hours. This quick turnaround is crucial for SMEs working under tight deadlines.
Do you require collateral for all your loans?
No, we offer unsecured loans for qualifying clients. Our assessment is based on your business viability and repayment capacity rather than just collateral.
What is LPO financing and how does it work?
LPO financing allows you to execute contracts without upfront capital. We finance your Local Purchase Orders, enabling you to fulfill contracts and get paid before repaying the loan.
Who is eligible to apply for your loans?
We serve micro, small, and medium-sized enterprises (MSMEs), entrepreneurs, traders, youth, women, persons with disabilities, and salaried employees. We especially welcome businesses underserved by traditional banks.
Where are your offices located?
We are based in Mombasa and primarily serve the coastal region. Contact us for specific office locations and to schedule appointments.
